    Frequently Asked
    Questions

    Everything you need to know about CAC registration in Nigeria.

    What is CAC and why do I need to register?+
    CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) is the Nigerian government body responsible for registering businesses and organizations. Registration gives your business legal recognition, lets you open a corporate bank account, bid for contracts, and builds trust with customers and partners.
    What's the difference between Business Name and LLC?+
    A Business Name (Sole Proprietorship) is owned by one person and is simpler and cheaper to register. An LLC is a separate legal entity requiring at least 2 directors, offering stronger legal protection and more credibility for businesses seeking investment or contracts.
    How long does registration take?+
    Business Name: 3‚Äì5 working days. LLC: 7‚Äì14 working days. Incorporated Trustees (NGO/Church): 3‚Äì6 weeks due to CAC's mandatory newspaper publication requirement. We keep you updated throughout the process.
    Do I need to visit any office in person?+
    No ‚Äî everything is 100% remote. Fill the form online, send documents via WhatsApp, and receive your certificate by email. You never need to visit our office or any CAC branch.
    What documents do I need for Business Name registration?+
    You need: (1) Valid government-issued photo ID ‚Äî NIN slip, International Passport, Driver's Licence, or Voter's Card. (2) Recent passport photograph with white background. (3) Proof of address ‚Äî utility bill not older than 3 months. That's it!
    Can my preferred business name be rejected?+
    Yes ‚Äî CAC rejects names already taken, too similar to existing names, or potentially misleading. That's why we ask for 3 name options and check availability before filing. You can also use our Name Search service to verify beforehand.
    What is Incorporated Trustees and who needs it?+
    It's the CAC registration for non-profit organizations ‚Äî churches, mosques, NGOs, charities, community groups, and professional associations. It requires at least 2 trustees, a constitution, and a newspaper publication notice.
    What happens after I submit the form?+
    You receive a unique reference number immediately. Our team contacts you on WhatsApp (usually within a few hours on business days) to collect your documents and confirm next steps. We then file with CAC and keep you updated until your certificate is ready.
    Do I need to file annual returns after registration?+
    Yes. CAC requires all registered businesses to file annual returns every year to remain active. Failure to do so attracts penalties and can lead to delisting. We offer Post-Registration Compliance services to handle this for you.
    Is my personal information safe with you?+
    Absolutely. Your personal details and documents are used solely for CAC registration and handled with strict confidentiality. We do not share your information with any third party other than the CAC portal as required for filing.
